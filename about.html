ABOUT NUMBER 10 SPORTS

INDIA'S INTEGRATED SPORTS ECOSYSTEM

Number 10 Sports is an integrated sports ecosystem company with a clear and ambitious vision: to transform how Indians engage with sports — from watching and listening to playing, learning, competing, and building lifelong connections with sport.

We bring together sports media, live streaming, infrastructure development, academies, events, experiences, stadium operations, and franchise management into one connected ecosystem designed for participants, partners, sponsors, communities, and investors.
